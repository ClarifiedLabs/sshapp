# Privacy Policy

SSH App does not collect, track, sell, or share personal data.

SSH connections are made directly between your device and the servers you choose.
We do not operate proxy servers and cannot see those connections.

By default, saved connections, known hosts, app settings, App Lock data, and SSH
credentials stay on your device. You can opt in to iCloud sync from the app's
iCloud Sync settings.

When Connections & Settings sync is enabled, saved connection metadata, known
hosts, app preferences, App Lock settings, and the salted App Lock passcode
verifier sync across your Apple devices through your own iCloud account. If you
also enable credential sync, saved passwords, eligible Ed25519 private keys, and
credential-protection settings sync through iCloud. Secure Enclave private keys
remain on the device where they were created. The developer cannot access data
stored in or synced through your iCloud account. Turning sync off keeps existing
iCloud copies separate from local data; the iCloud Sync screen provides a
separate action to delete those cloud copies without deleting local data.

We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking services.

If this policy changes, we will update this document and the App Store privacy
information before releasing a version with any data collection.
