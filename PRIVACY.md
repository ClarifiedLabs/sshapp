# Privacy Policy

SSH App does not collect, track, sell, or share personal data.

SSH connections are made directly between your device and the servers you choose.
We do not operate proxy servers and cannot see those connections.

Saved connections, known hosts, and app settings are stored on your device and
can sync across your Apple devices through your own iCloud account. Credential
sync is optional. When enabled, saved passwords, eligible Ed25519 private keys,
the app-lock passcode, and credential-protection settings can sync through iCloud.
Secure Enclave private keys remain on the device where they were created. The
developer cannot access data stored in or synced through your iCloud account.

We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking services.

If this policy changes, we will update this document and the App Store privacy
information before releasing a version with any data collection.
