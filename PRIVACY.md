# Privacy Policy

SSH App does not collect, track, sell, or share personal data.

The app stores any saved connections, settings, known hosts, and credentials only
on your device using iOS storage and keychain features. SSH connections are made
directly between your device and the servers you choose; we do not operate proxy
servers and cannot see those connections.

We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking services.

If this policy changes, we will update this document and the App Store privacy
information before releasing a version with any data collection.
